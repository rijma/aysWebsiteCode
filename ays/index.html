About us

We are offering the facilities of customer-to-customer and business-to-customer sales services. We will have headquarter in Kathmandu, Nepal. But we will be starting customer service office (branch office) in different cities of Eastern Development Region.

AYS Incorporation is a partnership firm of business organization. The business is operated by 6 partners. We have chosen this form of business organization as it will lead to increase credibility. It is even cost effective as each partner will be specializing in certain aspect of business and be able to contribute as an employee. Furthermore, the combination of skills of we, 6 partners has lead to widen the knowledge, creativity and contacts for our business.

Our Services

AYS plans to provide platform for the sellers and customers where they can buy and sell the used products as well as new products with efficient price and easy payment system. The vision of AYS Incorporation is to become an online market leader of Nepal.

Providing products such as fashion wear, books, watches, jewelry, electronic gadgets and many more. We serve through our website- ays.com, mobile application and also through physical branch. We provide facilities of C2C (Consumer to Consumer) as well as B2C (Business to Consumer) transactions. Besides, we also provide other services like affiliation service, direct advertisement, subscription service and many more which will also add to our sources of revenue. We also have planned to broaden and provide products and services in each and every corner of Eastern Development Region (EDR) through strategic alliance with courier companies and online payment facilitators

The main features of our business that differentiates us in the market and provide us competitive edge includes live chat solution for customers, expertise customer service personnel, physical customer service point despite being an online portal, multiple payment options and the motto of delivering what we promised. In the initial 5 years, we are planning to focus on the market of Eastern Development Region and our long term goal is to serve our services all over Nepal.
